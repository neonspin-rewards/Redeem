Before You Play

Welcome to NeonSpin. It’s a simple place where you can relax, explore, and enjoy the experience at your own pace without overthinking anything.

As you continue, you may notice rewards appearing from time to time. They aren’t fixed or guaranteed, since they depend mostly on luck along with a few basic conditions. That’s what keeps things a little more exciting.

The overall experience is designed purely for entertainment. Coins and EXP are part of the platform and help you progress and level up, but they don’t carry any real-world monetary value.

Your account and data are handled securely using Firebase Authentication. Your personal information stays private and is not sold or shared with third parties.

To maintain fairness, any misuse such as bots or system exploits may lead to restrictions or permanent bans. This helps keep the platform balanced for everyone.

If everything feels okay, you can continue and explore how it works. Most people understand it better once they try it.
